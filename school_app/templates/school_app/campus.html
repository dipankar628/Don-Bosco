A Campus That Inspires

Nestled amidst the lush greenery of Harmutty in the Lakhimpur district of Assam, St. Xavier's School boasts a sprawling campus that provides the ideal environment for learning and growth. The campus is designed to foster both academic excellence and holistic development, with modern facilities set against the serene backdrop of nature. From well-equipped classrooms to expansive sports grounds, every corner of our campus is designed to inspire young minds.

Our Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Technology-enabled classrooms with digital boards, projectors, and audio-visual aids for interactive and engaging learning experiences.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs providing hands-on experimental learning for students of all levels.

Computer Lab

Modern computer laboratory with high-speed internet connectivity, enabling students to develop essential digital literacy skills.

Library

A well-stocked library with thousands of books, reference materials, journals, and periodicals to cultivate the reading habit.

Sports Ground

Expansive sports fields for football, cricket, athletics, and other outdoor games, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

Chapel

A beautiful chapel within the campus for spiritual reflection, prayer services, and fostering moral and ethical values.
