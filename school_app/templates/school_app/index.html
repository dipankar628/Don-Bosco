Welcome to St. Xavier's School

St. Xavier's School, Harmutty, is a premier educational institution run by the Salesians of Don Bosco. Affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) with Affiliation No. 230010, the school offers education from Nursery to Class XII (Higher Secondary). Rooted in the Salesian tradition of Don Bosco, we believe in the integral development of every child — academically, morally, socially, and spiritually.

Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where students develop into responsible citizens equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to contribute meaningfully to society. With a dedicated team of educators, modern facilities, and a vibrant campus life, St. Xavier's continues to be a beacon of quality education in Lakhimpur, Assam.

From the Principal's Desk

Fr. Joshua

Principal, St. Xavier's School, Harmutty

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense joy to welcome you to the online home of St. Xavier's School, Harmutty. As a Don Bosco Institution, we are guided by the preventive system of education envisioned by our founder, St. John Bosco — a system based on reason, religion, and loving-kindness.

Our school strives to provide an environment where every child feels valued, inspired, and empowered to reach their fullest potential. We believe that education is not merely about academic excellence, but about building character, nurturing creativity, and instilling a sense of responsibility towards society and the environment.

I invite you to explore our website and discover the many opportunities that St. Xavier's School offers. Together, let us continue to build a brighter future for our children.

With warm regards and God's blessings,

Fr. Joshua
